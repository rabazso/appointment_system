## Sign Up Page
- **Registration:**
    - On this page the users can register to the website by entering their name, email and password
- **Password Confirmation:**
    - They need to confirm their passwords for safety reasons
- **Submission:**
    - If they filled out the form completely they can click on the "Create an Account" button
- **Existing Users:**
    - If the user has an account they can also choose the "Log In" option on the top right of the form to log in to their existing account
